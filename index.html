Abstract

Our solution consists mainly of getting rid of the greenhouse gases by utilizing their effect of trapping heat from the infrared radiation, this heat will be transported by aluminum (a good heat conductor), and it will produce mechanical energy using the difference in temperatures in the prototype that we are building. Our solution appeared to be very efficient and it met the design requirements chosen. Finally, we could design an efficient solution that can help in facing the pollution and climate change challenges.
